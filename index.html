Food-wine pairing

We will match your meal with the best wine for a succulent dine and wine experience. With the use of our fine-tuned algorithm we will recommend three types of wine for your meal. Our algorithm has been trained with Natural Language Processing and matches the characteristics of food with wine.

The algorithm quantifies the non-aroma and aroma properties of the meal and matches it with wines along the same dimensions. The ingredients of a meal are used to create a flavor profile. For example, a meal containing a hotdog, relish, pickles, and chili pepper has a significant non-aroma of saltiness. There is acidity from the relish, pickles and chili pepper. Lastly, the hotdog makes the flavor profile spicy, fat and full-bodied, but the profile ranks low on sweetness and bitterness.

Example with Swedish meatballs

The top 3 wines for Swedish meatballs:

  1. Sauvignon Blanc, San Antonio Valley, Aconcagua, Chile
  2. Red Blends, Red Blends, Macedonia, Greece
  3. Gruner Veltliner, Niederosterreich, Austria

Find Systembolaget near you

Systembolaget is a state-owned company with a statutory monopoly of alcohol in Sweden. Consequently, it's not evident what one should search for when trying to find liquor stores in a given location in Sweden.

We will help you find the closest Systembolaget based on your location. You need to allow location sharing by accepting the popup that appears after pressing the button in the section below. We will then help you find the top 3 closest Systembolaget to you, allowing you to save time and plan your perfect dinner efficiently.
